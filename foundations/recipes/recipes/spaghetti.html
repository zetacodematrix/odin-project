Simple Spaghetti

← Back to Odin Recipes

Description

This simple spaghetti recipe uses a basic tomato sauce and dried pasta. It’s quick, comforting, and perfect for a weekday dinner.

Ingredients

  • 200g dried spaghetti
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (400g) chopped tomatoes
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Grated cheese, to serve (optional)

Steps

  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil.
  2. Add the spaghetti and cook according to the package instructions.
  3. While the pasta cooks, heat olive oil in a pan over medium heat.
  4. Add the garlic and cook for about 30 seconds, until fragrant.
  5. Stir in the chopped tomatoes and oregano. Simmer for 5–10 minutes.
  6. Season the sauce with salt and pepper to taste.
  7. Drain the spaghetti and add it to the sauce. Toss to coat.
  8. Serve hot, topped with grated cheese if you like.
