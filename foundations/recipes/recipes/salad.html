Everyday Green Salad

Description

A quick green salad that works as a side dish or a light lunch. You can easily customise it with whatever veggies you have.

Ingredients

  • 2 handfuls mixed salad leaves
  • 1 small cucumber, sliced
  • 10 cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 small red onion, thinly sliced
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice or vinegar
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Steps

  1. Place the salad leaves in a large bowl.
  2. Add the cucumber, tomatoes, and red onion.
  3. In a small bowl, whisk together olive oil and lemon juice or vinegar.
  4. Season the dressing with salt and pepper.
  5. Pour the dressing over the salad and toss gently.
  6. Serve immediately as a side or on its own.
